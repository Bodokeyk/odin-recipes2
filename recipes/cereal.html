Cereal is one of the best possible ways to start the day ever

Lets start this recipe

Steps

First get your bowl and place it on the counter top or table. Then add cereal.
Now it is time to add milk! 1. Get milk 2. Remove cap 3. Pour onto cereal 4. put cap back on milk
Finally, add your spoon And enjoy your bowl of cereal!

Materials

  1. bowl
  2. Spoon
  3. milk
  4. A box of a cereal
